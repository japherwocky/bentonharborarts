Art Hops

Location: Arts District
Admission: FREE

Quarterly, the businesses in the Benton Harbor Arts District choose a Friday to host an Art Hop. It’s a family-friendly evening filled with an entertaining mix of art, music, dance, theater, culinary arts and more.

Come check out the multiple venues along the walkable Arts District streets. Meet your neighbors, talk with business owners and see impressive art. These popular events truly connect people with BHAA’s belief that “Art Builds Community.”

Harbor Market

Location: Dwight Pete Mitchell City Center Park

Harbor Market is an all local Farmer, Artisan and Vendor Market for the community of Benton Harbor, Michigan.

Concerts & Music

Open Stage

Location: The Livery

There’s so much talent in our area. On the first Monday of the month the BHAA showcases rising stars and regional favorites on this popular craft brewer’s stage with live performances by local musicians, poets and storytellers. It’s a chance for these gifted individuals to perform before an appreciative audience and draw inspiration from each other. A rotation of special hosts keep each show’s content rich, varied and lively. Paired with The Livery’s professional sound and lighting system, Open Stage is one of the best free, monthly events in the area!

Artoberfest

Location: Corner of 5th and Park Streets

ARTOBERFEST is a fun family-friendly festival! It's a mix of art, music, food, wine and beer sponsored by the BHAA and held every September outdoors in the Benton Harbor Arts District, rain or shine. ARTOBERFEST is a wonderful and diverse event that grows in popularity every year.
